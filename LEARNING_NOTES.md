### Q: "What's the difference between hashing and encryption?"

A: "Hashing is one-way - you can't reverse a SHA-256 hash back to the original data. Encryption is two-way - you can decrypt data if you have the key. In my FIM, I use hashing to verify files haven't changed, not hide their contents."
